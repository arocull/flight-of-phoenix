About The Game

Flight of the Phoenix is a fun side-scroller game for people of all ages. Take to the skies as a phoenix in search of its lost eggs. The journey will not be easy as there are many looming threats along the way. Dodge any threats you encounter and make it to the eggs! Good luck!

Controls: W/up arrow/space bar = jump
A/left arrow = move left
D/right arrow = move right
Jump off the ground, but be careful. You get x jumps in the air at a time before falling.
